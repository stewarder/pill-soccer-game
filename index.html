How to Play

Basic Gameplay

  • • Control your pill-shaped player with smooth physics
  • • Score goals by getting the ball into opponent's net
  • • First to reach the score limit wins the match
  • • Use walls and bounces to your advantage

Advanced Tips

  • • Master the art of ball control and positioning
  • • Time your jumps perfectly for aerial plays
  • • Collect power-ups for temporary advantages
  • • Practice different shooting angles
